## 👐 Building with hand gestures

The application interprets your hand movements to create shapes. Sit in front of your camera and ensure your hands stay within the frame.

*   **Move your hand**: This moves the point of orientation on the screen.
*   **Close your fist**: This selects a tool or grabs an existing object.
*   **Spread your fingers**: This releases an object or stops the current drawing action.
*   **Pinch gesture**: This creates a new vertex or point in the virtual workspace.

## 💡 Tips for success

*   **Maintain contrast**: Wear a long-sleeved shirt that contrasts with your background. This helps the AI track your hands with more accuracy.
*   **Adjust sensitivity**: Open the settings menu to adjust the tracking speed. If the cursor jumps around, reduce the sensitivity level.
*   **Calibrate**: If the application fails to see your hands, adjust your camera angle or move away from direct light glare.
*   **Restart**: If the display freezes, close the application and reopen it to reset the camera connection.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting common issues

If you face difficulties, try these simple solutions:

*   **Camera not detected**: Ensure no other applications like Zoom or Teams are using your camera at the same time. The camera can only connect to one program at once.
*   **Laggy video**: Close heavy applications like web browsers or video games while you build. This frees up memory for the gesture tracking engine.
*   **Application closes unexpectedly**: Update your graphics card drivers. Visit the website of your computer manufacturer to find the latest updates.
*   **Missing text**: If the interface looks strange, check your Windows display scaling. A setting of 100% works best for this application.

## 🏠 Frequently asked questions

**Do I need a VR headset?**
No. This software runs on your standard monitor and uses your webcam. You do not need extra hardware like headsets or specialized sensors.

**Is my webcam footage stored?**
No. The application processes your video feed in real-time. It does not record or save your video to your hard drive.

**Can I export my designs?**
Yes. Use the "Export" button in the main menu to save your 3D models to your computer for use in other software.

**Does this application require an internet connection?**
You only need an internet connection to download the initial installer. After you install the application, it functions fully offline.

**Can I change the background color?**
Yes. Visit the appearance tab in the settings menu to choose between dark mode or light mode for the virtual workspace.
